Why it matters

AI systems can access data, call tools, summarize sensitive material, and influence operator decisions. Security review needs to cover model behavior, integration points, trust boundaries, and abuse paths.

What gets assessed

  • Prompt injection and contextual manipulation
  • Connector, plugin, and tool execution safety
  • Secrets, logs, and retrieval data leakage
  • Approval workflows and output trust assumptions
  • Third-party AI platform governance risk
